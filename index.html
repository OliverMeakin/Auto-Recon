What it does

Finds real matches

Pairs and subset-matches transactions that net to zero within a configurable grouping (account, vendor, entity, project, or any custom key), with optional spillover logic across periods.

Enforces configurable rules

Supports many-to-one, many-to-many, and one-to-one matching, with rule-based constraints to prevent invalid combinations and surface edge cases for review instead of forcing matches.

Produces an audit trail

Writes match status back to the working sheet, appends cleared groups to an append-only audit log, and generates a detailed trace output for transparency and troubleshooting.
