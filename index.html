What it does

Finds real matches

Pairs and subset-matches transactions that net to zero within a configurable grouping (account, vendor, entity, project, cost center, or any custom key), with optional spillover logic across periods.

Enforces configurable rules

Supports one-to-one, one-to-many, and many-to-many matching, with rule-based constraints to prevent invalid combinations and keep teams in control.

Produces an audit trail

Writes match status back to the working sheet, appends cleared groups to an append-only audit log, and generates a detailed trace output for transparency and troubleshooting.

How it works

1) Configure your inputs

  • Select your grouping key (account, vendor, entity, project, etc.)
  • Choose amount columns (debit/credit/net) and optional memo/source fields
  • Set matching modes: exact, subset, tolerance, spillover (optional)

2) Apply match rules

  • Match within each grouping to net to zero
  • Apply constraints (type locks, source restrictions, cardinality rules)
  • Flag uncertain items for review instead of forcing matches

3) Review outputs

  • Status updates (Matched, Cleared, Tolerance Flag, Needs Review)
  • Append-only cleared log for audit support
  • Trace/debug sheet for transparency
Excel-native delivery: Ships as an Excel workbook + Office Script “app shell.” No new systems, minimal friction, and accounting teams can own the workflow.
